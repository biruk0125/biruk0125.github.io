## üìù FAQs

### 1. What is ARP spoofing?

ARP spoofing is a technique used by attackers to intercept network traffic. It happens when a malicious entity sends falsified ARP messages over a local area network (LAN). This results in the redirection of traffic.

### 2. How does this tool help?

This tool helps by monitoring network traffic and alerting users of potential ARP spoofing attacks, providing a layer of security.
